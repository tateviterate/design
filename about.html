philosophy

We exist to help our customers create their Petite Heimat… Spaces where You can be at ease with the complexities of identities that stubbornly elude categorization. Spaces that inspire contemplation and conversations, catalyzing positive social change. Spaces that invite users to live intentionally, in harmony with their values and choices.

mission

Petite Heimat is an online concept marketplace. We work with designers and artisans located around the world. Our hunt for the infinite wisdom contained in myths and traditions of all cultures guides our process of curation and creation. Every object and every interaction at petite heimat is treated as an experience. We try to make each experience worthy of our limited breaths.
